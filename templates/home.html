The 90s are considered by many to be a golden age of cartoons and TV Shows. They were weird, they were crazy, they were perfect. The 90s gave us classics like Hatim, Shaktiman, Scooby Doo, Tom & Jerry that are incredible creations that can never be equaled today. If you grew up in the 90s, you knew the joy of waking up early on a Saturday morning, racing to the TV, and spending hours fighting with your siblings over who got the remote.
